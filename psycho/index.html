Psycho

A Recreation of Psycho (1960)'s shower scene.

Watch the Film

This video is password protected. Please contact me to get the password.

This is a short practice film and a remake of the famous shower scene from Alfred Hitchcock's Psycho. I shot this film using an iPhone 13, and I handled the editing, sound design, and directing myself. The actor in the film is my girlfriend, and this was her first acting experience. This project was an excellent exercise for practicing rhythm and studying the use of a zoom lens to create suspense.
